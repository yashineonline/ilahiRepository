Shaykh Nishaat Ahmed Siddiqi

Being mad, is not bad. Being bad, will make you mad. We are mad, in love.

Love is the weapon that Satan cannot fight against.

Love is the highest form of friendship.

The lovers of Allah, love, without rules.

The Sufi path is the art of preparing the heart to receive.

Our goal is not to perfect ourselves, but to perfect our connection to Allah. In that connection, true peace is found.
